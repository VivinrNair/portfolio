Deltamere

An AI sustainability analyst for teams of one.

Inside most mid-caps, ESG compliance lands on one person. BRSR Core in one tab, a buyer’s 200-question audit due Friday in another, a CBAM email from a German customer nobody knows how to answer. The problem isn’t a lack of information — it’s that none of it is about your company.

What it does

Deltamere reads a company’s profile — listing status, sector, revenue, export markets, key customers — and filters roughly 200 regulations and frameworks down to the ten that actually apply. Each obligation becomes dated, ordered work on a living calendar; when a rule changes, the right item updates. Then it does the part nobody else does: drafting the disclosure narratives and pre-filling EcoVadis, CDP and buyer questionnaires from the stored profile.

My role

Conceived, designed and built end-to-end — the regulatory logic (BRSR, CSRD, CBAM, EUDR, EcoVadis, CDP), the product architecture, the copy, the landing page and the 30-second intro film below. It draws directly on running enterprise ESG at UST: every feature answers a pain I have personally sat inside.
